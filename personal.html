From Technical Foundations to Software Creation: My Journey in IT.

From a young age, I have been deeply fascinated by the world of ICT, but my professional journey truly began during my Expert IT Systems & Devices (MBO 4) studies. In 2023, I took my first steps as an entrepreneur by launching my own freelance business, allowing me to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application.

During my studies, I completed two distinct internship terms at Albeda College in Rotterdam as a Workplace Administrator (Werkplaatsbeheerder). Serving as the primary point of contact for both faculty and students, I gained extensive hands-on experience in:

  • Hardware & Software Support: Maintaining laptops and PCs while resolving complex Windows and Microsoft-related issues.
  • Incident Management: Utilizing TOPdesk to manage tickets, set priorities, and ensure rapid response times.
  • Crisis Management: Especially during critical exam periods, I learned to make independent decisions under high pressure and resolve acute technical problems instantly to ensure continuity.

While I have built strong expertise in system and network administration (Windows Servers, Packet Tracer, MySQL), I realized that my ambition goes beyond maintaining existing systems — I want to create them. This drive led me to pursue a Bachelor’s degree (HBO) in Software Development.

My goal is to focus entirely on my passion for technology. I am currently making a conscious transition with my freelance business into the heart of the IT sector to chase my dreams: building, managing, and perfecting innovative software and professional websites.
